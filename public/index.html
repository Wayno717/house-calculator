The Fees

Along side actually buying the house, there is a ton of other stuff you need to think about when moving. Including the 3 Major fees that come along with purchusing a property

Pre Purchusing Fees

  • Legal Fees, Solicitors can either charge a percentage of the house or a flat fee that can somewhere be in the range of 1.5k to 3k
  • If the House is a second hand property, you will need to pay for a Surveyor. They generally cost just under a 300-1k for a report
  • If the House is a new build, you will need a snag list. A snaglist is generally a list of items that your house will need that was not included in the original build.
  • Lastly, if the house is a second hand house, you willl need a to pay valuation fees before you can go for the mortgage. The price can be around 150-250

Post Purchusing Fees

  • Home Insurence, can range depending on insurence providers
  • Renovation & redecoration if the house is a second hand home, disregard if its a new build
  • Movers costs. Rent a van for a day can cost a couple a hundrad
